About Project Citylytics

Predictive Data Analytics and Quantitative Modelling of Catbalogan City’s Government Efficiency Indexing provides Intelligence Reports that is focused on the ten-point Kauswagan Katbalogan Agenda which is the Catbalogan City Mayor’s priorities relative to the major pain points of development.Now, these components are highly relative to what Catbalogan City needs in order to improve its performance and competitiveness based on the current rank and score (106th and 28.37/100.00, respectively) of the city according to the Cities and Municipalities Competitiveness Index 2022 Rankings of DTI. The following were identified based on the research works of the Catbalogan City Kauswagan Katbalogan Team.
